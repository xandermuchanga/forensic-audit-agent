# Responsible AI & Governance

## Governance Controls
- Read-only data access
- No automated financial actions
- Clear separation of agent responsibilities
- Evidence-based outputs only

## Explainability
Every finding includes:
- Reason for flagging
- Supporting evidence
- Clear audit logic

## Human-in-the-Loop
The system supports auditors by surfacing insights. All decisions and actions remain with human stakeholders.

## Responsible AI Statement
This solution was designed to align with enterprise governance standards, ensuring transparency, accountability, and safe AI adoption in financial audit workflows.
